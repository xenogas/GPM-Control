The available commands are:

  • Play / Pause - to start or stop the music
  • Next - to move on to the next track
  • Previous - to restart the current track, double click to move to the previous track
  • Rate - to give the track a thumbs up or thumbs down
  • Help - to bring up this window

Can't control Google Play Music from this webpage. Make sure that you are not in a privileged page such as about::debugging or the addons.mozilla.org domain.
